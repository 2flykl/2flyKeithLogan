THRU THE FIRE V4.1 — SECOND-LOOK DISCOVERY SYSTEM

WHAT CHANGED
- Each room begins with multiple visible savable items.
- One or two additional items are held in a hidden reserve.
- Only specially selected empty viewpoints can reveal reserve items.
- A reveal requires a revisit, a minimum amount of searching, and a weighted chance.
- Repeated revisits increase the chance, but a reveal is never triggered on every return.
- A hidden item becomes permanent after discovery.
- The player still saves at most one item per room, preserving the pressure of the decision.

REVEAL EQUATION
reveal chance =
  base chance
  + revisit bonus
  + room-search bonus
  + time-pressure bonus

The final chance is capped at 72%.
After enough revisits, the selected viewpoint guarantees its reveal so the feature is discoverable.

INSTALLATION
Copy the extracted assets and games folders into the root of the 2flyKeithLogan repository.
Merge and replace the existing V4 game files, then commit and push.
