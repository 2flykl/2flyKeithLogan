I WAS AWAY — V1.7 MUSIC + SMARTPHONE CONTROLS

BACKGROUND MUSIC
- The original MP3 was rebuilt without embedded album artwork/extra streams.
- The song is decoded into an AudioBuffer and played through the same Web Audio
  system already proven to play the game's sound effects.
- A native HTML audio player is retained as a fallback.
- The music remains at a steady background volume throughout gameplay.
- The parent experience iframe explicitly allows autoplay/audio.
- The MUSIC button shows loading, on, off, or retry status.

SMARTPHONE CONTROLS
- Transparent virtual joystick for movement and aiming.
- Large context action button:
  HOLD POWER → RELEASE → THROW → CATCH.
- Transparent buttons for orbit left, view, reset, and orbit right.
- Controls appear automatically on touch/coarse-pointer devices.
- Keyboard controls remain unchanged on desktop.

CACHE
- I Was Away assets and experience URLs use V1.7 cache-busting parameters.
- After deploying, perform one hard refresh: Ctrl + Shift + R.
