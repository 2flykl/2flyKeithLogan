I WAS AWAY — PAINTERFLY NO-STUCK CINEMATIC ENTRY

Sequence:
1. Click the I Was Away entry card.
2. Ambient wind and birds begin.
3. The overlay fades away on a fixed timer.
4. The existing PainterFly ENTER THE FIELD action is triggered automatically.
5. The song begins after the ambient opening and fades in.

The screen transition does not wait for the browser's audio promise.
A failsafe removes the overlay even if audio is blocked or delayed.
