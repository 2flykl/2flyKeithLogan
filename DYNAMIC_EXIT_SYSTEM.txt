THRU THE FIRE V4.2 — DYNAMIC EXIT + RISING FIRE

CORE FLOW
1. Search the room and optionally save one item.
2. The room does not end when an item is saved.
3. When four seconds remain, a hidden exit perspective activates.
4. Rotate left or right to find the exit perspective.
5. The exit view displays: PRESS UP TO EXIT ROOM THIS WAY.
6. Press Up before the timer reaches zero to advance.
7. Failing to find and use the exit causes an immediate game over.

EXIT SELECTION
- Regular rooms choose a randomized perspective.
- The system prefers an empty view that is 2–4 turns away from the player's current position.
- It avoids active objects and unrevealed second-look items when possible.
- The final hallway/front-entry room uses the front-facing doorway view.

RISING FIRE
- The overlay begins at zero height and zero opacity.
- It rises continuously according to elapsed room time.
- Its height is driven directly by the timer percentage.
- During the final four seconds, the exit phase and controls pulse for urgency.

IMPORTANT GAMEPLAY CHANGE
Saving an item no longer automatically advances to the next room. The player must still escape.
